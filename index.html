About CyberGlobe

Precision Engineering.
Enterprise Results.

CyberGlobe LTD is an independent infrastructure consulting practice delivering specialist network, security, and platform services to organisations that demand reliability, security, and operational excellence.

Whether you are modernising legacy infrastructure, migrating to the cloud, hardening your security posture, or managing a complex hybrid environment — we bring deep technical expertise and a pragmatic, vendor-aware approach to every engagement. We operate across the full infrastructure stack: from physical switching and routing to cloud-native platforms, from firewall policy to compute and storage architecture.

Our engagements are outcome-focused. We integrate with your team, align to your timelines, and leave your organisation in a stronger, more capable position than when we arrived.

What We Deliver

Full-Stack Infrastructure
Services

From layer 1 to the cloud management plane — we cover the breadth of your infrastructure with consistent depth and technical rigour.

Network Design & Architecture

Scalable, resilient network architectures for campus, data centre, WAN, and SD-WAN environments. From high-level design through to low-level implementation documentation.

  • Layer 2/3 design and topology planning
  • Data centre interconnect and fabric design
  • WAN, SD-WAN, and MPLS architecture
  • IP addressing and routing strategy

Network Support & Lifecycle

Proactive support, change management, and end-to-end lifecycle management to keep your network infrastructure secure, current, and performing optimally.

  • Operational support and troubleshooting
  • Firmware and software lifecycle management
  • Change and configuration management
  • Network health assessments and audits

Security Engineering

Infrastructure hardening, firewall architecture, zero-trust strategy, and security policy design across your entire estate — on-premises and cloud-native.

  • Next-generation firewall design and policy
  • Zero-trust network access (ZTNA)
  • Infrastructure hardening and compliance
  • Micro-segmentation and network access control

Compute & Storage

End-to-end design, deployment, and support for compute and storage infrastructure — from bare-metal servers to hyper-converged and virtualised platforms.

  • Server and HCI architecture and deployment
  • Storage area network (SAN) and NAS design
  • Virtualisation platform design (VMware/KVM)
  • Capacity planning and performance tuning

Platform & Cloud Services

Hybrid platform design and cloud integration across public cloud providers and private infrastructure — enabling seamless workload portability and operational consistency.

  • Public cloud architecture (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Private cloud and NSX-T platform services
  • Hybrid connectivity and cloud networking
  • Cloud governance and cost optimisation

Infrastructure Modernisation

Structured transformation programmes to retire technical debt, modernise aging infrastructure, and prepare your organisation for future scale and agility.

  • Legacy infrastructure assessment and roadmap
  • Technology refresh and migration planning
  • Network automation and DevOps integration
  • Documentation and knowledge transfer

How We Work

Our Engagement Model

A structured, transparent approach that gives you clarity, control, and confidence at every stage of your infrastructure project.

1

Discovery & Assessment

We begin by understanding your environment, objectives, constraints, and challenges. This includes reviewing existing architecture, talking to your team, and identifying the key risks and opportunities.

2

Design & Proposal

We develop a tailored solution design aligned to your goals and budget. This includes high-level architecture, technology recommendations, risk considerations, and a clear delivery plan.

3

Implementation & Delivery

Controlled, tested, and change-managed delivery. We maintain detailed records, conduct thorough testing at each milestone, and keep stakeholders informed throughout the implementation lifecycle.

4

Handover & Optimisation
